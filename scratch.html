Welcome to Alyssa's Acrylic Book of Mormons!


Our mission is to share the love of Jesus Christ with the world. God has a personal and unique relationship with each of His children. That's why these books of scripture have been hand-painted and customized just for you! These Book of Mormons are perfect gifts for baptisms, recent converts, departing missionaries, minstering assignments, or anyone else who could benefit from the good news of the gospel. Look through the gallery and create a design that reflects the love of Jesus Christ within the pages!


How It Works

These custom designs are limited only by your imagination. Submit artwork, photographs, and ideas to the artist to work with her one on one at creating the perfect cover! Get inspired by looking through the gallery or check out our Instagram Page for more ideas.

The Creation Process

Hardcover books are hand-painted with Master's Touch acrylic paint. Cover is then sealed with a clear, glossy, acrylic finish to prevent paint from chipping.
